Some Volume Renderers

VFleet is a distributed ray casting volume renderer being developed at the PSC.

BoB (for Brick of Bytes) is an alpha compositing volume renderer developed at the Army High Performance Computing Center in Minnesota. It works very well on Silicon Graphics machines with alpha compositing hardware (but will not work on non-SGIs).

VolVis is a general-purpose volume rendering package available from Arie Kaufmann's group at SUNY Stonybrook. It provides features emphasizing general computer graphics use.

VolPack is a volume rendering library featuring Lacroute and Levoy's new shear-warp algorithm.

netV is a distributed splatting volume renderer developed at SDSC.

There are plenty of others if you are willing to pay for them.
