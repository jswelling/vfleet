Same Data, Two Rendering Methods

The following two images represent the same set of fluid dynamics data. The field shown is vorticity; two vortices originally at right angles are interacting. One vortex passes through the top and bottom surfaces of the cube; the other passes through the left and right surfaces. The effect of the interaction is that the vortices end up partly wound around each other. The dimensions of the dataset are 48x48x48.

Here is an isosurface image of the vortex data, produced with the DrawP3D package.
